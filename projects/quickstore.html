Quick Store
Quick store is an app that makes life easy for an independent sellers to get up and running with their online store with the reliability of modern technologies.
Technology Stack
  • Mongo DB
  • Express.js
  • React.js
  • Node.js
Description
The main idea behind Quick Store is to provide an easy platform for the individual E-commerce sellers to conveniently get their own online store by installing Quick Store on their hosting platform and with very little editing like logo and the brand name and providing their payment gateway info and get their site up and running within no time.
Website Design
Was responsible for both the design and development of Quick Store website. The website was built using React for the front-end , Express(Node.js) for the Server and MongoDB as the Database.
Responsive Design
From the very start, the Quick Store website was designed and developed to be fully responsive. A responsive layout allows the website to resize appropriately across a wide range of devices including desktop computers, laptops, tablets (e.g. iPad) and smartphones
